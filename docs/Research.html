Welcome to My Research

By Mathematics' measured might, All hidden wonders break to light.

Mathematics is the universal language through which we decipher the patterns, structures, and fundamental principles that govern our world. From the elegance of pure theory to the power of applied solutions, mathematical inquiry opens doors to understanding nature's deepest secrets and humanity's greatest challenges. This space is dedicated to exploring the beauty and rigor of mathematics research; where abstract concepts meet concrete applications and where curiosity drives discovery.

Research Interests

Mathematical Biology

Modeling biological systems using mathematical frameworks, including gene regulatory networks and pharmacokinetics.

Semigroup Theory & Algebraic Combinatorics

Exploring algebraic structures and their combinatorial properties with applications to transformation semigroups.

Graph Theory & Network Science

Analyzing complex networks and their structural properties in biological and social systems.

Fixed Point Theory, Optimization & Machine Learning

Developing algorithms for optimization problems and applying machine learning to complex systems.

Stochastic Processes

Studying random walks and mixing times on various network structures and transformation semigroups.

Fluid Dynamics, Differential Equations & Numerical Methods

Modeling fluid-structure interactions and developing numerical methods for complex PDEs.
